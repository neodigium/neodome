**Autonomy:** 0-19%

*The client hires for one service. They experience one branch.*

### What Happens
- Single service delivery: website, workflow, Google setup, or lead system
- Quality of work plants the seed: *"What if more of my business ran like this?"*
- Client owns their credentials and data from day one

| Web Development | Live website with AI features |
| Smart Forms | Lead capture flowing on day one |
| Google Setup | Configured under THEIR account |
