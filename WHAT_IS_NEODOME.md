Most businesses run on a patchwork of disconnected tools:

```

│                                          and you're still doing  │
│                                          the work manually       │
│                                                                  │
│   "Need analytics"  →  Google + 3    →  Data scattered across    │
│       more tools       more tools       a dozen dashboards      │
│                                                                  │
│   Result: 5+ subscriptions, 3+ vendors, 0 ownership, full chaos  │
```

Every tool solves one problem while creating three more:

- **Vendor lock-in** — your data lives in their systems
- **Monthly drain** — $50 here, $100 there, $500/month in subscriptions
- **Knowledge debt** — only one person knows how it all connects
- **Zero ownership** — if that tool shuts down, you start over

The real cost isn't the money. It's the **ceiling** it puts on your growth.

## How It Works: Three Phases

### Phase 1 — Build
I build your complete digital infrastructure:
- Website (your domain, your design)
- Firebase project (your database, your auth)
- N8N workflows (your automations)
- Google Workspace (connected to your account)
- Client portal (you log in to your own portal to see your data)

**You get:** A live, working system. Hours, not months.

### Phase 2 — Managed Services
I run your systems monthly:
- Health monitoring (checks every 15 minutes, alerts if anything breaks)
- Workflow updates (new automations as your business grows)
- Analytics and reporting (Tree Score, KPI tracking)
- Support and maintenance

**You get:** Peace of mind. Your systems run themselves.

### Phase 3 — Full Ownership
You run NeoDome on your own hardware:
- LMStudio on your machine (private AI, no monthly API costs)
- N8N self-hosted (full control of all automations)
- Firebase on your own Google project
- I transition to advisor role only

**You get:** Complete autonomy. The system is yours forever.
