## Services by Phase

### Phase 1 — Foundation

*Delivered once. Your account. Your tools. Owned forever.*

| Service | What It Does |
|---------|--------------|
| **Web Development** | End-to-end web presence with AI capabilities |
| **Google Ecosystem Setup** | Configure once, own forever |

### Phase 2 — Intelligence Layer

*Live services running on NeoDome infrastructure.*

| Service | What It Does |
|---------|--------------|
| **BI Dashboard** | Complex data → clear, actionable visuals |
| **Smart Forms** | Intelligent data collection to your systems |
| **Lead Generation** | Pipeline that runs without manual effort |
| **AI Workflow Engineering** | Custom AI agents and automations |
| **AI Assistant** | AI that reasons, qualifies, and responds |
| **Security Sentinel** | Autonomous audit and hardening |

### Phase 3 — Full Autonomy

*Transfer everything to your own hardware.*

| Service | What It Does |
|---------|--------------|
| **Local Stack** | Full deployment on your dedicated hardware |
