### 🌐 Web Development

**Branch:** `web-setup` | PRESENCE Force | Phase 1

**What it is:**
A website built to your specifications — any type (static, dynamic, e-commerce, blog). Built on GitHub + Cloudflare Pages for global, fast, always-on hosting at ~$1/month.

**The problem it solves:**
Most small businesses pay $30-100/month for hosting that breaks, needs updates, and can disappear if the provider goes dark. This gives you a website that lives in your GitHub repo, deploys automatically, and costs almost nothing to run.

**3Angle:**

- **Portal:** Your live website, accessible globally, branded to your business
- **Repository:** Full source code — HTML, CSS, JS, config — in your GitHub
- **System:** Cloudflare Pages auto-deploys on every push. Zero maintenance.

**Dependencies:**
| Dependency | Why |
|------------|-----|
| GitHub account | Code lives here, you own it |
| Cloudflare account | Hosting, DNS, CDN |

**Autonomy Boost:**
- Code is yours
- Infrastructure is yours (Cloudflare)
- Deploy is automated but triggerable by you
- No vendor lock-in

---

### 📧 Google Ecosystem Setup

**Branch:** `google-setup` | CONNECTION + CONTINUITY Force | Phase 1 | **Yours from Day One**

**What it is:**
A complete configuration of Google Workspace tools — Gmail, Drive, Calendar, Sheets — set up professionally under your own Google account, integrated with your website and automation workflows.

**The problem it solves:**
Most businesses use Google tools informally — a personal Gmail, scattered Drive folders, no structure. This creates a professional, connected environment that works as a system.

**3Angle:**

- **Portal:** Your organized Google Workspace — business email at your domain, structured Drive, integrated Calendar
- **Repository:** Setup guide documents the configuration — you can maintain it yourself
- **System:** N8N integrates with Gmail, Sheets, Calendar. Connections run silently.

**This service is "yours from day one":**
Unlike other services that run on my infrastructure until Phase 3, Google Setup lives entirely under your Google account from the moment it's configured. There's nothing to transfer. It's already yours.

**Dependencies:**
| Dependency | Why |
|------------|-----|
| Google Account | Your workspace lives here |
| Domain (optional) | For custom business email |
| N8N (future) | For email automation |

**Autonomy Boost:**
- Everything is under your Google account
- No dependency on my infrastructure
- Setup documented for self-maintenance
